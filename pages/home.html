About Jack

From the age of 4, when my dad came home with a playstation instead of groceries, I have been immersed in tech.
With a video game programmer for a father, I quickly learnt the value of incremental growth, thanks to games like Gran Turismo, Metal Gear Solid, and Ridge Racer.
I also came to appreciate the reward in solving complex logic puzzles and the art of crafting an immersing story for a user.
To this day, I bring those lessons with me into each and every web project I create and contribute to.

As a teen growing up in Vancouver, Canada, with the iconic Stawamus Chief a short drive away, I discovered my passion for the sport of rock climbing.
Along my journey, I formed lasting connections with fellow climbers, and the sport became an integral part of my identity.
Driven by a desire to give back to the community, I spent my youth working in local climbing gyms, sharing my knowledge and expertise with the young climbers of Vancouver.
Through climbing, I honed valuable skills that extend far beyond the walls of a climbing gym. Creative problem-solving, effective teaching, clear communication, and the establishment of trust became the foundation of my approach.
These skills now seamlessly translate to my work and collaborations in any professional environment.

As a young man seeking personal challenges, I turned to the world of joinery, eager to test my skills and creativity.
With a natural eye for detail and an unwavering commitment to quality, I quickly immersed myself in the art of modern woodworking.
From meticulously applying mirror-like finishes to kitchen cabinetry to the delicate hand-sanding of cocobolo suitcase handles in my father's woodworking shop, I pursued the elusive balance between perfection and the inherent beauty found in the natural imperfections of wood as a medium.
Through my dedication, I developed the skills to craft custom pieces to tolerances as fine as 1/16th of an inch. And whether deciphering complex blueprints for multi-story houses or constructing an entire kitchen from scratch, I thrived on the mental challenges and unwavering attention to detail required.
While I continue to push the boundaries of my professional achievements, my unwavering focus remains on providing beauty and value to those who experience my work.
