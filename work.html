Jack

Born in England. Raised in Canada.

About Me

From the age of 4, when his dad came home with a playstation instead of groceries, Jack has been immersed in tech.
With a video game programmer for a father, Jack quickly learnt the value of incremental growth, thanks to games like Gran Turismo, Metal Gear Solid and Ridge Racer.
He also came to appreciate the reward in solving complex logic puzzles and the art of crafting an immersing story for a user.
To this day, he brings those lessons with him into each and every web project he creates and contributes to.

As a teen growing up in Vancouver Canada, with the iconic Stawamus Chief a short drive away, Jack discovered his passion for the sport of rock climbing.
Along his journey, Jack formed lasting connections with fellow climbers and the sport became an integral part of his identity.
Driven by a desire to give back to the community, Jack spent his youth working in local climbing gyms, sharing his knowledge and expertise with the young climbers of Vancouver.
Through climbing, Jack honed valuable skills that extend far beyond the walls of a climbing gym. Creative problem-solving, effective teaching, clear communication, and the establishment of trust became the foundation of his approach.
These skills now seamlessly translate to his work and collaborations in any professional environment.

As a young man seeking personal challenges, Jack turned to the world of joinery, eager to test his skills and creativity.
With a natural eye for detail and an unwavering commitment to quality, he quickly immersed himself in the art of modern woodworking.
From meticulously applying mirror-like finishes to kitchen cabinetry to the delicate hand-sanding of cocobolo suitcase handles in his father's woodworking shop, Jack pursued the elusive balance between perfection and the inherent beauty found in the natural imperfections of wood as a medium.
Through his dedication, Jack developed the skills to craft custom pieces to tolerances as fine as 1/16th of an inch. And whether deciphering complex blueprints for multi-story houses or constructing an entire kitchen from scratch, he thrived on the mental challenges and unwavering attention to detail required.
While Jack continues to push the boundaries of his professional achievements, his unwavering focus remains on providing beauty and value to those who experience his work.
